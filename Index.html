About KHM Textiles

Founded in 2022, khm Textiles has been providing premium quality fabrics to designers, tailors, and home sewers for over three decades. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the textile industry.

We source our materials from the finest mills around the world, ensuring that our customers receive only the best products for our projects.
